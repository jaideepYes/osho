7 All dreams must cease

< Previous | Contents | Next > ‌CHAPTER 7 All dreams must cease 27 October 1974 am in Buddha Hall REST AND UNREST DERIVE FROM ILLUSION; WITH ENLIGHTENMENT THERE IS NO LIKING AND DISLIKING. IF THE EYE NEVER SLEEPS, ALL DREAMS WILL NATURALLY CEASE....
