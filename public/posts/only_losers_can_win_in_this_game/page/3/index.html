28 God has no fixed address

< Previous | Contents ‌CHAPTER 28 God has no fixed address 31 October 1977 pm in Chuang Tzu Auditorium Prem means love, lolita means moved by, swayed by, possessed by, swinging, rocking: rocking and swinging in love. And let that become your very life-style. Remain in movement, remain in a kind of dance, and be swayed by the small things of life. They are not small! If we can be swayed by them, they are all extraordinary....

4 Hollow man or hollow bamboo

< Previous | Contents | Next > ‌CHAPTER 4 Hollow man or hollow bamboo 4 October 1977 pm in Chuang Tzu Auditorium Anand means blissful, bhagat means a devotee a follower on the path of love. There are three paths; one is of knowledge, of meditation, of purifying the intelligence. The second is of action, of service. And the third is of devotion, of love, of the heart. The path of action is body-oriented, the path of knowledge is mind-oriented, and the path of devotion is heart-oriented...

6 Divine Dictator

< Previous | Contents | Next > ‌CHAPTER 6 Divine Dictator 6 October 1977 pm in Chuang Tzu Auditorium Deva means divine and suraj means order: divine order. To be in that state one has to be possessed by god. One has to lose oneself more and more, one’s ideas, one’s mind, one’s manipulations, one’s control. There can be two orders in a human being. Either man orders himself – he is his own government – or god becomes his government and he simply follows....

7 Be a honey bee

< Previous | Contents | Next > ‌CHAPTER 7 Be a honey bee 7 October 1977 pm in Chuang Tzu Auditorium Deva means divine, madhukar means honey bee. And it is a very very pregnant symbol in the East – the honey bee. It represents freedom. It never becomes attached to any flower; it simply goes from one flower to another, collecting honey. But it is never attached, it remains unattached....

9 Re-gem-ber yourself

And the diamond is there; we just have to search for it. Everybody brings it with their birth. God never sends anybody poor; he provides for the whole journey. But he keeps the diamond in such a hidden place in you that sometimes it takes many lives to discover it....
