< Previous | Contents | Next > ‌CHAPTER 39 9 September 1979 pm in Chuang Tzu Auditorium Prem Alice. Prem means love; alice means truthful one, noble one, cheerful one. Bliss is the ultimate goal of life, and the people who become blissful, naturally become messengers of God; not that they have to do something – their very blissfulness becomes a message to the world, their very cheerfulness is heard far and wide....
